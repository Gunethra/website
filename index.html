About the fest

Following up on the success of our first edition, Zozimus is returning on 12th and 13th March 2022 to celebrate a gathering of literary enthusiasts and groups from all over the country. Through the literary fest, we desire to connect like-minded college students and others who wish to be a part of this exciting scene. The fest will be sprinkled with a myriad of events such as quizzing, debating, writing, and speaking competitions coupled with engaging informal literary events like scrabble tournament and online treasure hunt.

Building up to Zozimus is the Online Litfest, inviting submissions via our website and social media for a set of quirky, byte-sized events. Kicking things off, we hope to carry forward this idea and invite you to join us on this journey.

A Tale of Two Halves

Budding writers world over have started stories without ever finishing them, while others have come up with the perfect ending without knowing how to begin

12th March


Register here!

A Tale of Two Halves will test your abilities as a writer, by having you not only begin a story based on a prompt during the first half, but also by having you finish off someone else’s story during the second half.

Rules

  1. Participants will have to join a meeting where a collection of prompts will be released.
  2. After choosing their prompts, they will be given an hour to write down half a story based on their chosen prompt.
  3. Once the hour is up, they will have to submit their story to the judges (by Google Forms/ some other submissions platform).
  4. After a 15–30-minute break, they will be randomly allotted the first half of one of their competitors. The second hour of the contest will be spent with them finishing off the half they have been given.
  5. Winners:
    • Best Overall Story: 2 Winners
    • Best First Half: 1 Winner
    • Best Second Half: 1 Winner
  6. This is an individual competition, participating as a team is heavily discouraged.
  7. Contestants will not be informed of who got allotted their first half, or whose second half they’ve been allotted.
  8. Winners will be announced on the same day.

Sweetheart

“Only handwritten love letters knew what love really felt like The blue ticks & time stamps have no clue what patience in love really means!”
~21st century Romeo

19th Feb - 10th March


Register here!

Everyone has had their share of first love(s), be it the boy-next-door, the pretty girl who always sat on the first bench, or even the cute teacher in 2nd grade ;) If you want to pour your heart out and pen down your emotions for that special one, this is the perfect event for you! That first secret peek of their faces, that first conversation in the hallway (which you were trying to make for ages), those cute childish giggles… too many to name! This is your best chance to let that special one know how you feel for them.

Rules

None hinders thy love except for a few rules described below to moderate the happenings!

  1. Since this is an online event, only ‘typed’ submissions will be accepted via the Dare2Compete Platform.
  2. Love knows no language, but we do! We’ll accept all entries in English, Hindi (or even Hinglish) because we all know at the end of the day, it’s the emotions that matter, not the multisyllabic words!
  3. Plagiarism? A STRICT NO! If you fail to abide by this, there will be immediate expulsion from the event.
  4. After all, you have so much to express about your ever-lasting love! But we would appreciate it if you scale down to 350-450 words (50 words here and there would be alright coz ur in love ¯\_(ツ)_/¯ )
  5. Please soften your language and curb your free speech on the real names being used.
  6. Refrain from making insensitive remarks or using abuse and foul language of any form. It will lead straight to disqualification.

Evaluation

Although everyone's a winner in love, there can be only a single winner in this competition. Our winner will be decided by judging your entries according to the passion and beauty you can portray your feelings and emotions. Though there will be a single winner who will win our hearts, the best parts of your tales will have an HONOURABLE mention on social media.

Feel free to contact any of us in case of a query. We’ll be more than happy to reach out!! Adios!

2 Line Terror

“Where there is no imagination, there is no horror.”
-Sir Arthur Conan Doyle


4th March - 10th March


Register here!

The name says it all. Unleash the horror within and come up with a two-liner that sends chills down the spine of the reader! Remember to keep it as compressed and blood-curdling as possible. There's no limit to how scary you can go, so give us all you've got!

Rules
